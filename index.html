RULES

Play rock, paper, scissors with a friend or your worst enemy to break a tie, make a decision, or kill time rather than each other.

Why?

Disputes are time consuming and often biased. Rock, Paper, Scissors is the fairest way to solve any disputes by leaving it up to chance, fate, destiny, luck, and you're ability to stare your opponent straight in the eyes and read his mind.

How to play

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • If you and your opponent choose the same option, it's a tie, and no one will get hurt
